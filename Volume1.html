Volume 1

Volume 1 of our Archives marks the inaugural year of our journal, offering a compelling snapshot of the early research and foundational studies in the field of science and technology. Published in 2025, this volume features a pioneering collection of research papers that laid the groundwork for future advancements. It includes a diverse array of topics ranging from breakthrough experiments in quantum mechanics to innovative approaches in artificial intelligence and sustainable technology. Each paper from this inaugural volume reflects the cutting-edge work and emerging trends of its time, providing a valuable historical perspective on how initial inquiries and methodologies have influenced subsequent research. Exploring Volume 1 allows readers to appreciate the origins of key scientific discussions and trace the development of ideas that have contributed to the evolution of the field.
