Submit Paper

AJRST extends a warm invitation to academicians, researchers, and industry professionals to submit their manuscripts for publication consideration. Our platform is dedicated to disseminating high-quality research that advances the frontiers of science and technology. Before submitting your work, please take a moment to review our submission guidelines and ensure your paper aligns with our ethical standards.

Submission Guidelines
  • Manuscript Preparation: Your manuscript should be formatted according to AJRST’s template, which includes specific instructions on structure, references, and formatting. The manuscript template can be downloaded from the link below. Authors can submit their manuscripts in their own standard Word format, and our associate editors will convert them to AJRST format if necessary.

  • Originality: Submissions must be original works that have not been published elsewhere and are not under consideration by any other publication.

  • Language: Manuscripts should be written in clear, concise English for a global audience.

  • Figures and Tables: High-quality figures and tables should be included (if any) to illustrate key points and support your findings.

  • Supplementary Materials: Authors are encouraged to provide supplementary materials such as datasets, code, or additional documentation that enhance the value of their research.

Peer Review Process
  • Initial Screening: Manuscripts are initially screened for compliance with submission guidelines and overall suitability for AJRST.

  • Expert Review: Qualified experts in the relevant field rigorously evaluate each manuscript for its scientific merit, originality (plagiarism not more than 30%), clarity, and significance.

  • Feedback: Author receive feedback from the chief editor regarding the acceptance or rejection of their submitted manuscripts. If accepted, the corresponding author are required to complete the final procedures, which include signing and submitting the AJRST copyright form and providing proof of payment.

Ethical Standards

AJRST upholds strict ethical standards to ensure the credibility of published research. Authors must confirm that their work complies with ethical considerations such as informed consent, animal welfare, conflict of interest disclosure, and data integrity.

Ready to Submit?

If your manuscript meets our guidelines and you’re ready to contribute to the body of scientific knowledge, please proceed to our submission form for authors. We look forward to reviewing your work and potentially featuring it in AJRST.
