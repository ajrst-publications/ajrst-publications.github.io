Editorial Board

Explore into the Editorial Board of Academic Journal of Research in Science and Technology (AJRST), where expertise meets innovation. Our board consists of esteemed scholars, researchers and academicians from diverse disciplines within science and technology. Led by the Editor in Chief, they uphold rigorous standards of scholarly excellence and contribute to advancing cutting-edge research. Discover the individuals behind our peer-reviewed process, driving the journal's commitment to quality and impact in the global academic community. The board members are more than gatekeepers of academic rigor; they are active contributors to the global academic community, shaping discussions and driving impactful research forward. Their commitment to advancing knowledge and addressing critical challenges underscores AJRST's dedication to making a significant impact on the scientific landscape.

Through their collective efforts, the Editorial Board of AJRST not only evaluates the merit of submissions but also cultivates a culture of collaboration and excellence. They play a pivotal role in ensuring that the journal remains a trusted source for groundbreaking research, thereby contributing to the advancement of science and technology worldwide.
