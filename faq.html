Frequently Asked Questions

Academic Journal of Research in Science and Technology (AJRST) is a globally recognized, peer-reviewed, open-access journal that covers a wide range of scientific disciplines. Below, we answer common questions about submission guidelines, review processes, publication timelines, and more.

Yes, the Academic Journal of Research in Science and Technology is an International, fully peer-reviewed, open-access publication. It adheres to the policy of double-blind peer review. Papers from any area of science and technology, including management, social science, agriculture, medicine, and other fields of study, are accepted for publication in AJRST. Engineers, academics, scientists, academicians, researchers, and students from all over the world are welcome to submit research papers and review articles to AJRST.

In the 'Submit Paper' section, you will find a form where you can enter your information and upload your paper for submission. Should you encounter any issues, you have the option to send the paper directly to the editor via Email at ajrst.publications@gmail.com

Yes. Download the manuscript form from the journal's "Submit Paper" button. You also have the option to submit your article in Word format and our associate editors will convert your article to the standard AJRST format if accepted.

No, there is no deadline for online paper submissions, an author can submit an article to any journal at any time. However, the standard delivery date for a particular issue/month is the last day of that month.

After receiving the author's manuscript, the peer review procedure, including the double-blind review, generally takes approximately 2 to 4 days, though this can vary by paper. Once the review is complete and the paper is accepted, it is typically published online within 24 hours, provided that the author has submitted the necessary final procedures, including the copyright form and payment proof.

Articles accepted for publication may submit their copyright forms after receiving notification of acceptance by Email. The author can download and print the copywriting form, fill it out, scan it and send it though Email.

The best articles/papers are selected based on the magazine's score for that month. Reviewers assign scores to our accepted journals (out of 100) that are published in AJRST. These grades are based on the importance of the paper, its degree of plagiarism and its content. If the plagiarism report is less than 30%, we give the paper to our reviewers, where it is reviewed in a double-blind process to be approved or rejected.
