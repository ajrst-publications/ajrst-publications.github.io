Author Guidelines

The Academic Journal of Research in Science and Technology (AJRST) invites researchers, academicians, and industry experts worldwide to contribute original research papers, review articles, case studies, short communications, and technical notes across various disciplines of sciences and technology. AJRST is dedicated to fostering rigorous academic research and facilitating intellectual discourse within the global scientific community. We encourage submissions that introduce innovative findings, offer theoretical insights, showcase methodological advancements, and demonstrate practical implications for both academia and industry.

Submission Requirements:

  • Page Limit: Manuscripts must be at least 1 page long and can extend up to a maximum of 25 pages.
  • Author Limit: Each manuscript can include a minimum of 1 author and a maximum of 8 authors.

Our peer-review process is designed to be fair and efficient, managed by a distinguished editorial board comprising experts from diverse scientific fields. Accepted manuscripts are promptly published online, ensuring rapid dissemination and visibility of your research outcomes.

Authors are required to adhere meticulously to AJRST’s manuscript formatting template, which details the structure, citation style, and overall presentation guidelines. Submissions must be original and should not be under consideration by any other publication concurrently. Manuscripts should be written in clear, concise English, accessible to an international readership. Authors are encouraged to include high-quality figures and tables judiciously to emphasize key findings. Furthermore, supplementing research with additional materials such as datasets, code, or documentation is recommended to enhance scholarly value.

AJRST serves as a platform for scholarly exchange, aiming to advance scientific knowledge and encourage interdisciplinary collaborations. We invite researchers to contribute to our journal and join us in promoting excellence in scientific research and technological innovation.

  1. Manuscript Format: All research published in AJRST must adhere to the AJRST manuscript format. This format ensures consistency and facilitates proper citation practices, which are essential for academic integrity and efficient publication workflows. To assist authors in meeting these standards, we provide a downloadable AJRST manuscript template on our website. This template includes guidelines for formatting text, citations, references, and other essential elements required by our journal. In case authors encounter difficulties in formatting their manuscripts according to AJRST guidelines, we offer a solution: authors can submit their manuscripts in plain word format. Upon submission, our associate editors will convert the manuscript to AJRST's standard format before the publication process begins. This service aims to streamline the publication process and ensure that all accepted manuscripts meet our journal's formatting requirements seamlessly.

  2. Submission of Manuscript or Research Paper: Submitting a manuscript or research paper to AJRST is straightforward. Authors can use the "Submit Paper" section on our website, where an online submission form is available for easy uploading of documents. Alternatively, manuscripts can be submitted via Email to ajrst.publications@gmail.com, your paper will be sent for review after submission. Upon submission, each manuscript undergoes a thorough peer-review process, which typically takes between 24 to 48 hours from the time of submission. Authors can find detailed information about our peer-review process under the "About" tab on our website, ensuring transparency and understanding of our evaluation procedures.

  3. Completion of Final Procedures (Copyright and Payment): Upon acceptance of a manuscript for publication in AJRST, authors are required to complete final procedures to finalize the publication process. This includes submitting a duly signed copyright form and providing proof of payment. The copyright form ensures that AJRST obtains the necessary rights to publish the research, thereby protecting the intellectual property of both the authors and the journal. Authors can download the copyright form from our website and submit it via email after their paper has been accepted. Proof of payment should also be submitted alongside the copyright form. Detailed instructions for completing these final procedures will be communicated to authors through email upon acceptance of their research paper.
