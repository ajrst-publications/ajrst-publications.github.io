Privacy Policies

This Privacy Policy outlines how AJRST collects, uses, and safeguards your personal information when you engage with our website, which is powered by GitHub, and when you make submissions through Google Forms. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of both GitHub and Google Forms to understand their data handling practices.

  1. Information Collection and Use: AJRST collects personal information based on your interaction with our services. For example, when you submit manuscripts through Google Forms, we collect personally identifiable information, including your name, email address, designation, department, institution, state, and country. This information is utilized exclusively for the purposes of manuscript processing, communication, and improving our journal services. We may also use your contact information to provide updates about our journal or related services, but only if you have given consent to receive such communications.

  2. Publication of Papers: When a paper is published, it will only include the following details: your full name, designation, department, institution, state, and country. We do not include email addresses or other contact details in the published papers. These personal details are used strictly for internal communication purposes within AJRST and will not be disclosed to any third parties.

  3. Data Sharing and Disclosure: AJRST is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not share, rent, or sell your personal information to third parties, except as necessary to deliver the services you request or with your explicit permission. We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, address safety threats, or enforce our policies. In cases where we share data with service providers or partners, we ensure that they uphold similar privacy standards and agree to safeguard your information.

  4. GitHub-Powered Platform: Our website operates on GitHub, a reputable platform that provides the infrastructure and services for our site. GitHub employs robust privacy policies and security measures to protect data hosted on its platform. We appreciate GitHub's commitment to data protection and are confident in their efforts to ensure a secure environment for your information. We work diligently to ensure that our data practices align with best practices for privacy and security.

  5. Data Security: AJRST employs a range of technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. We continuously review and update our security practices to address emerging threats and vulnerabilities.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
