AJRST | Academic Journal of Research in Science and Technology

The Academic Journal of Research in Science and Technology (AJRST), your gateway to a world where science and technology converge to spark innovation and inspire change. As an internationally recognized, peer-reviewed, open-access online journal, AJRST is dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in scientific research and technological advancements. Our platform is meticulously designed to facilitate the global dissemination of high-quality research across a multitude of scientific fields, from the fundamental to the applied. Powered by GitHub, our website ensures a robust and scalable infrastructure that supports seamless access and collaboration for researchers worldwide.

In the ever-evolving landscape of scientific inquiry, AJRST stands as a beacon of knowledge, guiding researchers through the complexities of modern science and technology. We are committed to fostering an inclusive academic environment that encourages diversity of thought and collaboration among scholars from all corners of the globe. Our journal serves not only as a repository of groundbreaking research but also as a forum for intellectual exchange, where ideas can be shared, challenged, and refined.

At AJRST, we understand the transformative impact that research can have on society. We strive to ensure that every article we publish meets rigorous standards of methodological soundness and contributes meaningfully to its respective field. By offering a platform that supports both established and emerging voices in the scientific community, we aim to catalyze progress and drive forward the collective understanding of humanity.

We invite you to explore our latest articles, which encompass a wide array of topics and reflect the cutting-edge of scientific discovery. Whether you are looking to submit your latest paper or simply wish to stay abreast of recent developments, AJRST is your trusted partner in academic publishing.

Join our growing community of researchers who are committed to shaping the future through science and technology. Submit your paper today and take your place among those leading the charge in academic excellence. Together, let’s embark on a journey of discovery that transcends boundaries and sets new benchmarks for what is possible.

Why AJRST

  • AJRST is a Journal that is International, Peer-reviewed, and Open Access with Low Article Processing Charge.
  • AJRST has been included in Google Scholar, PuBMed, Web of Science, ResearchGate, Academia, Zenodo, and several other indexes.
  • The process of publishing a paper is quick and straightforward and each author receives their own E-Certificate from AJRST.
  • Monthly award for best paper authors, featured in our special issue with E-certificates and cash prizes.
  • Looking for a high-visibility platform to promote your articles? Check out our open access journal database!
  • Papers can be submitted by authors at any time using the online submission platform.
  • The paper will be published in the current issue right after acceptance.
  • AJRST invites original and well-crafted research papers from all academic disciplines within science and technology.
  • Authors have the ability to download their complete paper at any given time.
  • Author's inquiries will be addressed within a 24-hour timeframe.
