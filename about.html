About AJRST

The Academic Journal of Research in Science and Technology (AJRST) stands as a beacon of knowledge dissemination in the scientific and technological realms. As an online publication journal, AJRST is deeply committed to fostering scholarly communication and collaboration within the global academic community. Established with the vision of creating a dynamic platform for researchers, academicians, and industry experts, AJRST prides itself on its commitment to quality and integrity in every article it publishes. Powered by GitHub, our website benefits from a robust, scalable infrastructure that ensures a seamless and reliable user experience, supporting our mission to disseminate research effectively.

Our platform not only facilitates the sharing of groundbreaking research findings but also encourages scholarly discourse and innovation across various disciplines. With a user-friendly interface and open-access model, AJRST ensures that valuable research is accessible to a wider audience, thereby accelerating the advancement of science and technology.

Our Mission: At AJRST, our mission transcends beyond mere publication; we aim to be catalysts for innovation and discovery. By providing a reputable outlet for researchers to present their work, we contribute to the scientific tapestry with threads of knowledge and expertise. Our rigorous editorial standards are the cornerstone of our commitment to excellence, ensuring that every paper published undergoes a comprehensive peer-review process by esteemed members of the academic community.

We are dedicated to bridging the gap between research and practice, making cutting-edge findings available to practitioners, educators, policy-makers, and the curious minds who seek to expand their understanding of the world.

Research Area: AJRST champions a broad spectrum of research topics that span theoretical concepts, current status reviews, empirical research, standards development, practical applications, experimental methodologies, usage in diverse applications, and insightful case studies from industrial settings. We welcome contributions from both academia and industry in all tracks, fostering a symbiotic relationship that enriches our journal’s content.

Our publication covers an extensive range of subjects within the following domains:

  1. Natural Sciences: Delving into the mysteries of physics, unraveling chemical interactions, exploring earth sciences’ complexities, and understanding biology’s wonders.

  2. Engineering and Technology: Advancing frontiers in all engineering branches, pushing boundaries in computer science, and pioneering technology development.

  3. Medical and Health Sciences: Enhancing knowledge in medicine, nursing excellence, pharmacy innovations, and public health advancements.

  4. Agricultural Sciences: Promoting sustainable agriculture practices, advancing food science research, exploring forestry’s challenges, and veterinary science breakthroughs.

  5. Social Sciences and Humanities: Analyzing economic trends, shaping education’s future, understanding psychological phenomena, exploring sociological aspects, interpreting legal frameworks, revisiting history’s lessons, and celebrating the arts’ diversity.

Editorial Board Guiding: AJRST is our distinguished editorial board composed of eminent experts from various scientific disciplines. Each board member brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table, ensuring that our journal upholds its academic integrity. They are instrumental in supporting impactful research dissemination while mentoring emerging scholars in their publishing endeavors.

Our editorial board members are not just gatekeepers of quality; they are advocates for ethical research practices and are committed to fostering an environment where integrity is paramount. Their dedication is reflected in their unwavering support for authors throughout the publication process.
