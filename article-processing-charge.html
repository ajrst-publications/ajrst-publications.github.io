Article Processing Charge

We are committed to supporting researchers from around the world in sharing their findings through our open access platform at AJRST. To continue providing high-quality publishing services and to sustain the advancement of scientific knowledge, we are introducing an Article Processing Charge (APC) for all articles published in AJRST.

APC Structure:

  • For Indian Authors: ₹420 per published paper
  • For International Authors: $5 per published paper

AJRST publishes journals under the Open Access model, ensuring that articles are freely accessible to anyone, anywhere in the world, and available for download via our reading platform. This model supports the global dissemination of knowledge but involves various costs. The APC helps cover these expenses, including Editorial Manager, Technical Support, Article Preparation, and Marketing Efforts.

Additional Benefits:

  • Free E-Certificates: Authors will receive electronic certificates confirming their publication at no extra cost.
  • Indexing Services: Articles will be indexed to boost its visibility and citations.

Important Details:

  • Fees Apply Per Published Paper: The APC is charged for each paper that is published.
  • Fees Apply After Editorial Approval: The APC applies after your manuscript is approved for publication.
  • No Fees Before Acceptance: Charges apply only after your manuscript passes our editorial review.

We look forward to receiving your submissions and contributing to the advancement of scientific knowledge through open access publishing.
